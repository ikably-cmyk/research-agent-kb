## Key Points

- Systematic review evaluating oral LLLT (photobiomodulation) for oral mucositis in children and young people with cancer undergoing chemotherapy.
- 14 studies (n>416 children) included in narrative synthesis; 5 studies (n=380) included in meta-analyses.
- Results suggest LLLT may reduce severity of oral mucositis and level of oral pain, but further RCTs are needed to confirm.
- Vast variation in trial protocols across included studies limits definitive conclusions.
- Insufficient blinding between LLLT and sham/control groups led to strong risk of performance bias.
- 75 studies (2712 patients of all ages) demonstrated minor and infrequent adverse reactions to LLLT.
- LLLT appears to be a safe therapy; evidence for efficacy as prevention or treatment of oral mucositis in children with cancer remains insufficient.
- PROSPERO registration: CRD42018099772.
